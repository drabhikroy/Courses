You will get the most of out this class if you:

  1. engage with the readings and materials,
  2. use R to complete any data wrangling or analysis,
  3. ask for help immediately after giving it (whatever it is) a good faith effort, and
  4. keep an open and honest line of communication with me and your peers if possible.

Below is a list of items considered to be class related tasks.

R Training

Details on training modules can be found on the Data Camp page.

R Projects

You will need to complete two R projects to be submitted in Rmarkdown. Details about these projects will post approximately three weeks before the due date.

R Tasks

On most weeks, you will be asked to submit an R related task. These must be turned in by Wednesday at 11:59 PM on the ecampus Submission Portal.

Meetings

Starting after the fourth week, we will need to meet on a biweekly basis to discuss project parameters and to give you an opportunity to ask questions. These sessions are scheduled for 15 minutes but can typically be adjusted if needed.

Final Proposal and Project

Details about the final project will post within two weeks.
