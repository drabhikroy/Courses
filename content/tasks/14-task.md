## Some Additional Resources

1.  [*Topic Modeling*](https://www.tidytextmining.com/topicmodeling.html) is a very well-written introductory chapter from the book **Text Mining with R: A Tidy Approach**

2.  [Topic Modeling with R](https://ladal.edu.au/topicmodels.html) is much deeper dive into the area from the *Language Technology and Data Analysis Laboratory* at the University of Queensland

3.  [Topic Modeling in R With tidytext and textmineR Package](https://medium.com/swlh/topic-modeling-in-r-with-tidytext-and-textminer-package-latent-dirichlet-allocation-764f4483be73) is a thorough and very readable walkthrough
